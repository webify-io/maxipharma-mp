MaxiPharma: Pioneering Pharmaceutical Excellence

MaxiPharma Consultancy (Pty) Ltd. is a South African company, providing consulting services in the areas of all type Pharmaceutical dosage from development, and manufacturing. We are also now pleased to offer non-GMP development trial services in our new, small-scale laboratory. Currently we are equipped for direct blending, high shear granulation, fluid bed granulation, tablet compression and encapsulation. We also have induction heat sealing capabilities for hand packaged bottles.

"Getting your product to the market faster and keeping it there."

MaxiPharma is more than just a Pharmaceutical Research and Development or CDMO. We work with your team as an extension of your own laboratory and/or commercial operations, allowing you to leverage MaxiPharma’s technical leadership in quick melts development and benefit from MaxiPharma’s comprehensive expertise from quick melts concept to commercialization.

For drug product design and development, the MaxiPharma team uses strategic planning, Quality by Design (QbD), and risk-based approaches to help you identify “Go/No Go” decisions earlier and avoid unnecessary costs and delays.

For commercial manufacturing, MaxiPharma has built on nearly a decade of regulatory, pre-clinical, and clinical manufacturing experience specifically for solid dosage form pharmaceuticals under principal leadership.
