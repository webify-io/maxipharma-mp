Why We Are Different

MaxiPharma is more than just a Pharmaceutical Research and Development or CDMO. We work with your team as an extension of your own laboratory and/or commercial operations, allowing you to leverage Maxi Pharma’s technical leadership in quick melts development and benefit from Maxi Pharma’s comprehensive expertise from quick melts concept to commercialization.

For drug product design and development, the MaxiPharma team uses strategic planning, Quality by Design (QbD), and risk-based approaches to help you identify “Go/No Go” decisions earlier and avoid unnecessary costs and delays.

For commercial manufacturing, MaxiPharma has built on nearly a decade of regulatory, pre-clinical, and clinical manufacturing experience specifically for solid dosage form pharmaceuticals under principal leadership.

We will deliver your high-quality product faster, more cost-effectively, and get your product to the market faster and keeping it there.
