EFFERVESCENT TECHNOLOGY:

EFFERVESCENT CAPLETS:

MaxiPharma Laboratory specialises in the development, technology transfer and production of effervescent products. We offer expertise throughout the product life cycle, from development to commercialisation of your effervescent product ideas, including analytical testing and packaging.

Our development staff have successfully developed and brought effervescent products to market for our clients. Products include Multivitamins effervescent tablets, analgesic, antipyretic and anti-inflammatory.

Dissolve in water for a pleasant tasting drink that can deliver large quantities or multiple APIs in a single dose.

Active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) or supplements delivered through oral dosage forms may encounter the risk of low absorption, but this may be overcome by administering the drug in liquid form. However, because many APIs show limited stability in liquid form, effervescence application has provided a good solution; drug dissolution and immediate consumption enables faster, and more complete absorption compared to conventional tablets.
