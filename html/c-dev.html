Microbiological Standards:

Microbiological tests are tools that can be used along the entire beverage supply chain to reveal the absence or presence of certain microorganisms and indicate the effectiveness of sanitation.

Your microbiological standards, or micro program, should consider government regulations, beverage production guidelines and best practices, and the quality and safety standards you set for your product as part of your business strategy.
