Why MaxiPharma Services?

MaxiPharma Consultancy (MPC) is in Johannesburg South, South Africa. With many years of expertise, and an experienced team who upholds an outstanding regulatory record, we deliver flexible, value-added manufacturing solutions that are designed to accelerate and simplify your products pathway to market.

MPC was established as an independent business unit during 2010 in recognition of the need to bring an independent team of experts together, which understands the entire drug development and registration process.

We have an understanding of what you as a customer require. As such, we are committed to meeting the needs of our clients. We provide unbiased guidance, advice and offer our combined expertise in R&D, Manufacturing, and complete regulatory support.

We have a combined 71 years of industry experience where 400 products were developed and launched under principal leadership, more than 140 Dossier submissions and more than 100 approvals. We have experience in Research & Development (R&D), Manufacturing, cGMP, Validation & Qualifications and Quality Assurance field. We are a full-service provider of technical services for the pharmaceutical industry. Our company has positioned itself to assist the pharmaceutical industry with the best practices applicable to it.

Focused on your installed base and production environment, we offer you dedicated support and expert advice. Our portfolio ranges from feasibility studies, consulting on packaging design and pack styles, solution, and R&D services. Our highly qualified professionals are experts in their respective fields. They will be happy to adapt this service to your individual requirements.
